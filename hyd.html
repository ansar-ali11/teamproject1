About Hyderabad

Hyderabad, the capital of Telangana, is known for its rich history, stunning architecture, and delicious cuisine. With its historic landmarks like the Charminar and Golconda Fort, the city boasts a unique blend of old and new. The vibrant culture is reflected in its festivals, music, and dance. Hyderabad is also a hub for information technology and business, often referred to as "Cyberabad." Enjoy the warmth of its people, the aroma of its spices, and the beauty of its gardens, including the famous Lumbini Park and Necklace Road.

Must-Visit Places in Hyderabad

Charminar

This 16th-century mosque is an iconic symbol of Hyderabad. Explore its stunning architecture and vibrant markets, where you can shop for traditional bangles and pearls.

Golkonda Fort

A historical fortress with fascinating acoustics and panoramic views. Don't miss the light and sound show that tells the story of this majestic fort and its rulers.

Hussain Sagar Lake

A beautiful artificial lake featuring a massive Buddha statue at its center. Enjoy boating, and explore the scenic surroundings, especially at sunset.

Ramoji Film City

Ramoji Film City is a sprawling film complex that offers a glimpse into the world of cinema. Rajiv Gandhi International Airport

One of the busiest airports in India, offering modern facilities and a gateway to the city. Known for its efficiency and hospitality, it connects Hyderabad to the world.

Shilparamam

A crafts village that showcases the traditional arts and crafts of India through exhibitions and workshops. It's a perfect place to pick up souvenirs and learn about the local culture.
